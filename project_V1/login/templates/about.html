How to use BUDDYUP...

 

Step 1: Login to Buddyup. If you are not a member, please click on Signup to get registered to Buddyup.


Step 2: Add your friend's Twitter Username and Email.


Step 3: Once you add your friend, a pending sign would show up.


Step 4: Wait for your friend to accept your request and give access to get added to your following list.



Step 5: Once the access is granted, pending changes to View Trend. Click on this button to view your friend's Twitter Analysis.



Congratulations!! Now, you can successfully view your friend's Tweets and get a notification when something negative is tweeted.

Step 6: Enjoy Buddyup!

About Trend

1. The Trend shows your friend's overall trend on the basis of their tweets.

2. The Trend is based on the date of the tweets and the overall Sentiment Level of the tweets for that day.

3. All the tweets are segregated based on the day they were tweeted.

4. Blue circles represent the Sentiment Level of tweets of the day.

5. Hover over the circle to view the Date of tweets and their sentiment scores.


6. A summary of the trend shows the sentiment (Positive, Negative or Neutral) of your friend's tweets in the past 10 days.



7. You can click on a circle to view a detailed analysis of the day.

8. You can view the tweets of the day and the most frequent words in a wordcloud

9. Time of the tweet in a scatter plot.
